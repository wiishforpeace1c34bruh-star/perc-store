How it works

Up in under three minutes.

01
Download and extract
Download perc.zip and extract it anywhere on your Windows machine. No installer, no system changes, no admin required.
02
Sign in to the loader
Run perc.exe. The loader authenticates your license and lists your active modules. Free access to the OSINT module is always available.
03
Select a module and go
Click any module to launch it in a dedicated terminal window. Each one walks you through what it needs and shows results immediately.
