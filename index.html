How it works

Up in under three minutes.

01
Download and extract
Download perc.zip and extract it anywhere on your Windows machine. No installation wizard, no system changes.
02
Sign in to the loader
Run perc.exe. The loader authenticates your license and lists the modules you have access to. Guest access is available for the OSINT module.
03
Select a module and go
Click any module to launch it in a dedicated terminal window. Each module walks you through what it needs and shows results immediately.
